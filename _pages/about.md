I am a Ph.D. candidate in Public Policy and Political Economy at UT Dallas. I research public policies that incentivize innovation using causal inference techniques from applied microeconomics. Currently, for my doctoral thesis, I'm studying policies impacting the technology market for antibiotics to combat the public health threat of Antimicrobial Resistance.
